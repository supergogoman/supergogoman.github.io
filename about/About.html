About

Sharon Singh was born in Southampton, England.
She attended the Birmingham School of Acting where she was awarded the Sir John Geilgud Bursary Award and the Susie Shelton award for outstanding voice work.

Drama School credits include; Beth (A Lie of the Mind), Celia (As You Like It), Dr.Branom/Ensemble (A Clockwork Orange), Soul (Hydriotaphia), Reggie Flutey (The Laramie Project), and Isabella (Measure for Measure).

Making her stage debut as Erin in Holes (The Arcola Theatre), Sharon has gone on to work in various productions including; Lizzy (Love Bitten, Dan Allen Films), Hamlet (Hamlet Peckham, The Bussey Building), Lavinia (Titus Andronicus, Greenwich Theatre), Montano/Bianca (Othello, Greenwich Theatre), Jennifer (The Misanthrope, Ecole Superieur), Sabiya Kabir (Doctors, BBC).
